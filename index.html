MEMBER ASSOCIATE TRAINING GUIDE

Your 5-Day Journey to MA Excellence

WELCOME TO SCENTHOUND!

Congratulations on joining the Scenthound team! Over the next five days, you'll learn everything you need to become a confident and capable Member Associate. This guide outlines what you'll be learning each day. Use it to prepare, take notes, and reference throughout your training. By Day 5, you'll be certified and ready to work independently!

DAY 1

Drop Off and Pick Up Fundamentals

What You'll Learn Today:

  • How to safely handle dog handoffs
  • Kennel management basics
  • Understanding the Scenter Experience Map
  • How to use menu boards and the knowledge wall
  • Proper lobby etiquette
  • How to navigate MyTime schedule
  • Complete drop off and pick up processes
  • Member engagement techniques
  • Introduction to membership sales
  • House rules and policies

Drop Off Process

Tools You'll Be Using:

  • Employee Experience Map
  • Brand DNA
  • Scenter Experience Map
  • Laminates (quick reference guides)
  • Daily Checklists
  • MyTime Symbol Quick Guide
  • Knowledge Wall
  • FOSA SOP Binder

The Drop Off Process (What You'll Practice):

  1. Stand and greet the customer by name when they walk in, then greet their pet
  2. Quick assessment - observe the dog's demeanor and condition
  3. Confirm services and estimated completion time
  4. Confirm contact information
  5. Transfer and take the dog to the back
  6. Weigh the dog
  7. Place the dog in their kennel

Today's Activities:

  • Review the Member Experience Map and front-of-store (FOS) tools with your trainer
  • Watch your trainer demonstrate a drop off
  • Practice through roleplay with your trainer
  • Shadow several real drop offs
  • Debrief with your trainer about what you observed

Pick Up Process

Tools You'll Be Using:

  • SCENT Check reports
  • Scenter Experience Map
  • Laminates
  • Knowledge Wall
  • Educational Trifolds

The Pick Up Process (What You'll Practice):

  1. Stand to greet the pet parent
  2. Go over their dog's SCENT check results
  3. Make relevant product and service recommendations
  4. Check out and rebook their next appointment
  5. Bring their dog out

Today's Activities:

  • Learn house rules and policies
  • Get introduced to MyTime booking system - learn basic navigation and how to book appointments
  • Learn about mid-appointment texts and tracking FOSA tasks
  • Watch your trainer demonstrate a pick up
  • Practice how to discuss SCENT scores
  • Learn how to recommend products and add-on services
  • Practice rebooking appointments
  • Shadow several real pick ups
  • Debrief with your trainer

END OF DAY 1 - REFLECTION

Take a moment to think about what you learned today. What felt natural? What do you want to practice more? Jot down any questions for tomorrow.

DAY 2

Reinforcing Fundamentals & FOSA Training

What You'll Learn Today:

  • Reinforce drop off and pick up processes
  • Dive deeper into kennel management
  • Learn how to handle telephone inquiries
  • Master the FOSA system
  • Practice booking appointments
  • Learn to make effective calls to New Leads and Active Trial Leads

Morning Review

You'll Review and Practice:

  • Drop off basics - then roleplay to reinforce
  • House rules review
  • Booking in MyTime review
  • Pick up basics - then roleplay to reinforce

FOSA System Training

New Tools You'll Learn:

  • FOSA SOP Document
  • FOSA App
  • Telephone Inquiry Process
  • Breed Book

What You'll Learn About FOSA:

  • How we use FOSA, MyTime, Zoho, email, and scripts to ensure smooth operations
  • Practice logging in and out of all systems until you're comfortable
  • Learn the Telephone Inquiry process
  • Go through the FOSA Procedure guide step-by-step
  • Watch your trainer make a call to a New Lead
  • Practice booking appointments using test accounts (you'll delete these after practice)
  • Practice leaving professional voicemails that sound natural, not robotic
  • Make your first New Lead call!
  • Learn how to handle Active Trial Leads
  • Watch your trainer make a call to an Active Trial Lead
  • Practice with your trainer through roleplay
  • Practice booking more appointments (repetition builds muscle memory!)
  • Make your first Active Trial Lead call!
  • Learn the other FOSA tasks and complete them if there are enough in the system
Remember: Engaging conversations are our bread and butter, but being able to leave solid voicemails without sounding like a robot is an important skill. Practice makes perfect!

END OF DAY 2 - REFLECTION

Review the day with your trainer. Celebrate your wins, reflect on challenges, and highlight what you learned. FOSA can feel overwhelming at first - that's completely normal!

DAY 3

Building Independence & Trial Appointments

What You'll Learn Today:

  • Continue building confidence with FOSA
  • Practice booking appointments independently
  • Handle phone calls with less assistance
  • Master drop-off and pick-up processes
  • Learn the special Trial appointment process

Morning Practice - More Independence

What to Expect:

  • Your trainer will shadow you (not the other way around!) during morning drop offs and pick ups
  • You'll debrief after each interaction to get feedback
  • You'll practice keeping track of the schedule for mid-appointment texts and prompt pick ups
  • During downtime, you'll roleplay more complex scenarios

Complex Scenarios You'll Practice:

  • Scenario 1: "My dog is rabies exempt"
  • Scenario 2: "I need a haircut TODAY"
  • Scenario 3: "I have a severely matted doodle and I just want to come in for a bath. I can only come in at 5:30 pm, when is your next available?"
Get comfortable with not knowing everything! The goal isn't to have all the answers memorized - it's to build confidence. You may not know the answer, but you know who to ask or where to look it up. Things are always changing, and Seek Growth is one of our core pillars. If we ever feel like we know everything, we're failing on that count. Being open and transparent is part of what makes Scenthound unique!

FOSA Practice

What You'll Do:

  • Your trainer will shadow you through several FOSA tasks
  • You'll debrief after each call to reflect and improve

Trial Drop Offs - THE MOST IMPORTANT SKILL

New Tools You'll Learn:

  • Trial Guide
  • Trial Drop Off Guide
  • Knowledge Wall (deeper dive)
  • Laminates
Trials are how we grow! This is how we get new members. These appointments set expectations for future visits and are critical to our success. Pay close attention!

Trial Drop Off Process:

  1. Stand and greet the customer by name, then greet their pet
  2. More thorough assessment - take your time to observe the dog
  3. Start with the waiver
  4. Explain services and emphasize towel dry, give them the Future Member brochure
  5. Confirm shampoo type
  6. Confirm time and contact info
  7. Ask if they have any questions
  8. Transfer and take the dog to the back
  9. Weigh the dog
  10. Place in kennel

What You'll Learn:

  • How trial drop offs differ from regular member drop offs
  • Why trials are so important to our business
  • How to confidently enforce lobby rules
  • How to conduct thorough assessments that set a positive tone
  • Roleplay with your trainer
  • Shadow a real trial drop off if possible

Trial Pick Ups - WHERE THE MAGIC HAPPENS

Tools You'll Use:

  • Trial Pick Up Guide
  • Houndswell Product Sheets
THIS IS THE BIGGEST DEAL! Trial pick ups are how we convert new customers into members. Every step matters!

Trial Pick Up Process:

  1. Greet warmly
  2. Deliver your pitch
  3. Go over SCENT Check - start with 5's and work down, explain what each score means
  4. Transition - "We don't want to see trends like these continue, that's why we have our monthly memberships"
  5. Recommend membership - use the Goldilocks method, HIGHLIGHT KEY 4 IN PLUS
  6. Closing pitch - "We loved Sparky so much, we're gonna waive that registration fee for you today"
  7. Let. It. Simmer. - Be quiet. Don't fill the silence. They will let you know if they have questions
  8. Once they decide (signed up or declined), check out and rebook
  9. Bring their dog out

Your Goal by End of Week:

  • Have your own pitch perfected and natural
  • Understand why each step is important
  • Practice the pitch with your trainer multiple times
  • Roleplay trial pick ups
  • Shadow a real trial pick up if possible

END OF DAY 3 - REFLECTION

Today was a BIG day! You learned about trials - the lifeblood of our business. Review what you learned and practice your pitch before tomorrow.

DAY 4

Training Wheels - Building Confidence

What You'll Learn Today:

  • Work more independently with drop off/pick up
  • Handle FOSA and phones with confidence
  • Master booking appointments
  • Learn to overcome objections
  • Handle challenging situations and conflict resolution

Handling Challenging Situations

New Tools You'll Learn:

  • Workdrive resources
  • Membership Cancellation SOP

What You'll Learn:

  • Cancellations are handled by managers only - learn what to do if someone requests a cancellation when a manager isn't available (let them know a manager will follow up ASAP)
  • Learn the concept of "pain flushing" and the power of silence
  • How to get to the root of WHY someone is objecting
  • How to address objections and handle them professionally
  • Practice through multiple roleplay scenarios with feedback

Shadow Shift - You're in the Driver's Seat

What to Expect:

  • Your trainer will explain the Independent MA Certification and what each requirement means
  • You'll work relatively independently while your trainer shadows and fine-tunes your skills
  • Your trainer will find teaching moments throughout the shift
  • You'll start working through your certification checklist
  • Your trainer will coach on areas needing improvement
  • Your trainer will praise what you're doing well

END OF DAY 4 - CERTIFICATION CHECK-IN

  • Review what you learned today
  • Review your certification progress with your trainer
  • Go over expectations for tomorrow's final evaluation
  • Know what you've already passed and what you need to demonstrate tomorrow

DAY 5

Training Wheels Off - Certification Day!

Today's Goals:

  • Demonstrate your independence as an MA
  • Complete your MA Certification
  • Celebrate your success!

Certification Day

What Today Looks Like:

  • Review the certification requirements one more time
  • Your trainer will shadow you all day, being as hands-off as possible
  • Your trainer will provide support only when needed
  • You're encouraged to use your tools to find answers to questions
  • During downtime, you'll do intensive roleplay practice:
    • Ring ring - phone scenarios
    • Save me - handling difficult situations
    • Sell me - membership pitches
    • Teach me - explaining services
This is YOUR day to shine! Show everything you've learned over the past four days. Trust yourself - you've got this!

END OF DAY 5 - FINAL EVALUATION

  • Review everything you learned this week
  • Final temperature check with your trainer
  • Sign off on your certification OR create a plan for additional practice next week
